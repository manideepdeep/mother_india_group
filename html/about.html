FAQ

What types of properties do you offer for sale or rent?

We offer a wide range of properties, including apartments, houses, commercial spaces, and land. Whether you're looking for a place to call home or an investment opportunity, we have options to suit your needs.

How do I list my property for sale or rent on your website?

Listing your property is easy! Simply visit our "List Your Property" page, fill out the required details, upload photos, and submit the listing. Our team will review your listing, and once approved, it will be visible to potential buyers or renters.

What areas do you cover?

Mother India Real Estate operates in multiple cities and regions across India. You can search for properties in various locations, and our website allows you to filter results by city, neighborhood, or region.

A reset link will be emailed to you.

Do you provide assistance with property financing or legal matters?

While we don't directly offer financing or legal services, we can connect you with trusted partners, such as banks, mortgage brokers, and real estate lawyers. We have a network of professionals who can assist you with the financial and legal aspects of your real estate transaction.

Are there any fees associated with using your website?

Browsing and searching for properties on our website is entirely free. However, there may be fees associated with listing your property for sale or rent. Please refer to our pricing and packages page for details on any associated costs.
